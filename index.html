Ocellaris Clownfish

The Ocellaris Clownfish may be one of the aquarium industry's most popular marine fish. Its beautiful orange body dressed with white bands outlined in black instantly distinguishes the Ocellaris Clownfish.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Temperament: Peaceful
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Minimum Tank Size: 20 gallons

The Captive-Bred Ocellaris Clownfish is very hardy and more accustomed to conditions found in home aquariums. Therefore, it makes a great choice for novices and seasoned aquarists alike. The Captive-Bred Ocellaris Clownfish can also be kept with a variety of other captive-bred clownfish, if introduced into the aquarium at the same time.

Banggai Cardinalfish

The Kaudern's Cardinal, also known as the Banggai Cardinalfish, is a very attractive fish with a simple yet sophisticated color scheme and marking..

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Temperament: Semi-aggressive
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Minimum Tank Size: 30 gallons

Its overall body coloration is a shimmering silver, highlighted with pearly white spots. Bold, black stripes along the body accentuate the long fins to elegant effect. The Banggai Cardinal is relatively easy to breed in the aquarium setting. Once spawning has occurred, the male carries the eggs in his mouth to protect them.

Firefish

The Firefish is one of the more popular fish in the marine hobby. It is a magnificent fish with brilliant coloration, a unique body shape, and unparalleled personality.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Temperament: Peaceful
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Minimum Tank Size: 20 gallons

The Firefish Goby is also heralded as a sweet-tempered fish with lots of personality. In the wild, the Firefish Goby is usually found in groups hovering over the reef, harvesting planktonic food that drifts by in the current. The docile, Firefish Goby is timid and in the wild, each will have a "bolt-hole" into which it quickly ducks when threatened.
